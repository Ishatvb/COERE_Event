Centre of Excellence for Renewable Energy

Welcome to the Center of Renewable Energy at Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology!
Our center is a dynamic hub for pioneering research, education, and innovation in the field of renewable energy. Committed to sustainability and environmental stewardship, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the global energy landscape.

Mission:

Our mission is clear—to advance renewable energy solutions through cutting-edge research, provide transformative education, and actively engage with our communities to promote sustainable practices.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Research Excellence:
    We lead impactful research initiatives, exploring innovative technologies and methodologies to address the world's growing energy challenges.

  • Education and Training:
    Empowering the next generation of leaders, our educational programs equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to drive change in the renewable energy sector.

  • Community Outreach:
    We believe in making a difference beyond our campus. Through collaborations and projects, we actively contribute to creating a positive environmental impact in local communities.

Facilities:

Our state-of-the-art facilities provide an ideal environment for hands-on learning and experimentation, fostering a culture of innovation and sustainability.

Collaborations:

Collaboration is key to our success. We actively seek partnerships with industry leaders, research institutions, and governmental bodies to drive impactful advancements in renewable energy.


Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology

Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology (JUET), Guna has been established vide Government of Madhya Pradesh gazette extra ordinary No.3 of 2010 dated 29th April 2010 as a private university in the State of MP under the provisions of MP Niji Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam 2007. The university has been notified by the UGC under section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956 and Accredited by NAAC with Grade “A+” in 2nd Cycle in 2023 and Grade "A" in 1st cycle of Accreditation in 2016. This University is sponsored by Jaypee Group. It was earlier Jaypee Institute of Engineering and Technology, Guna, established in the year 2003 based on the MOU signed between Jaiprakash Sewa Sansthan (a not-for-profit trust) and the Government of Madhya Pradesh with an aim of becoming a Center of Excellence in Engineering and Technology in MP.

Policies

Here are your policies

  • Presentation requirement

    To ensure proper registration and publication of Technical Publications and Technicalities, the following requirements must be met:

    1. Author Registration:

      At least one author must be registered at the Full Conference Author rate.

    2. Paper and Presentation Limit:

      One author registration can cover a maximum two (2) technical or presentation-only submissions along with two (2) additional poster presentations.

    3. Presentations:

      If author intends to present more two (2) papers or presentation-only at the podium, they request a co-author register.

    4. Poster Presentations:

      All authors of a presentation are students, the author can register at appropriate student rate, provided do so before the Advanced Registration deadline.

    5. Student Registration Requirements:

      Student registration rates are exclusively available to full-time undergraduate and graduate students who have not yet received their Ph.D. Post-doctoral researchers cannot register as students. Proof of student status, such as a copy of the student identification card or a letter from the institution's registrar, is required to qualify for the student rate.

    6. Presenter Attendance Policy:

      In order for a paper to be published in the official archival proceedings, it must be presented at the conference by an author or co-author. These proceedings are registered with the Library of conference and abstracted and indexed.

    7. Conference Format:

      Technical conferences will be conducted as in-person events.

    8. In-Person Requirement:

      Video and/or zoom presentations will not be accepted as substitutes for in-person presentations during conferences.
